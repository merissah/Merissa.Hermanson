CSEPP

This simple little site was a much needed redesign from a messy, confusing, outdated version. I had to keep their old logo, but otherwise I had the freedom to design the rest of it the way I wanted to. The main goal of the site was to register users for the CSEPP courses located in three different states. I decided to keep everything as simple as possible, using the three main locations as the focal point of the site. Since the training covers using many of the free google products available, at the request of the client, I used Google forms for the registration. I had to embed the forms into the site so I could control the styling and then create my own javascript validation, since the embedding broke the Google form validation. At the request of the client I also embedded Google maps for each location.
