Can I Eat This?

Montezuma’s revenge, Delhi belly, or travelers’ diarrhea—whatever you call it, an illness from unclean food or water can ruin your international trip. This app is designed to prevent travelers’ diarrhea and other illnesses. You simply select the country you’re in and answer a few simple questions about what you’re thinking about eating or drinking, and Can I Eat This? will tell you whether it’s likely to be safe. The client wanted a design that was colorful and fun. It also had to look similar to their sister app TravWell. I went with a bright, happy color scheme, and an international map as a subtle background. Since this app was really simple I felt using the photographs as the navigation would be the best experience for the user.
