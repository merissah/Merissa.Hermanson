A little ABOUT me

My name is Merissa Hermanson. I am a designer specializing in print design, web design, web development, and illustration. I also have some experience with 3D modeling and animation. I am passionate about creating engaging, and visually appealing images. Whether it’s a painting, photograph, website, animation, illustration, or even a simple post card. I make it look good. I originally started out in fine art and have a bachelor’s degree in painting. Shortly after I found that I had a passion for graphic design as well and spent over a year working as a desktop publisher. For the past three years I have been mostly designing websites and web apps.
