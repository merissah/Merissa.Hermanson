NRC Online Training

These Online trainings are a conversion of the classroom trainings the Nuclear Regulatory Commission had been doing for years. The wanted something clean, modern, engaging, and anything but text heavy. I tried to keep the design simple and broke up the text with a lot of white space, typography, and graphics. This first training included 32 different modules so I was part of a team. I handled the overall design, graphics, most of the animations, and helped a little with the development. This project is still under development and has not been cleared. Please do not redistribute.
