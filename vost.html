VOST

This simple little site was created for emergency management & disaster recovery volunteers to self-register and allow gathering of training, experience and criteria needed to place them. I designed and built the site on my own. The client requested that I use Google forms to collect the all of the information. I only used color for the five main places the user should go to register, steering them exactly to where they needed to go. I really wanted the images and the overall feel of the home page to emphasize how important volunteering really is and motivate them to help. I also think the black and white gives the destructive images a more dramatic feel. I used CSS animations and a parallax scrolling effect to give it some added interest.
