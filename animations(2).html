Animations

Most of these animations were created using Autodesk Maya 3D software. The Nuclear Regulatory Commission needed various animations for the online training we developed for them. Most of them are different particle interactions which were better represented in 3d while others were created in After Effects. This project is still under development and has not been cleared. Please do not redistribute.
